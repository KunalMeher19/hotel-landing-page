Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of single rooms are generally between 37 sq m to 45 sq m.


Family Room

A parlour or living room is connected with one or more bedrooms. (A room with one or more bedrooms and a seperate living space.)


Duplex Room

The most expensive room provided by a hotel. Usually, only one suplex suite is available in one single hotel property . Similar to the normal suites, a duplex suite always has one or more bedrooms and a living space with a strong emphasis on grand in-room decoration, high-quality amenities and supplies, and tailor-made and services(e.g. personal butler during the stay.)
